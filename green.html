Green Wedding

The concept of green weddings is very close to our heart moreover couple these days love the indulgence of nature in their wedding. Whether it’s the dazzling hays of forest or a setup near the wildlife there’s something extra ordinary about the green weddings plus they are a great way to go Eco- friendly in your wedding all the major décor happens to be the nature itself.

Jim Corbett

This is probably the first location which comes in everyone’s mind when we think of a green wedding and it deserves to be there too because it’s India’s first wildlife reserve. With more than 200 varieties of flora and fauna, the national park is one best place where you want your adventurous wedding to take place. Coming to the accommodation part there are multiple luxury resorts that hosts complete wedding and that not about it! Hence it’s a one stop destination for all your adventure wedding fantasies if you want your wedding to take place in this beautiful jungle, make sure to have most of it.

Kerela

How can we not add this in here? There’s a reason why Kerala is called “Gods Own Country” thanks to its soothing beaches, beautiful backwaters, and stunning hill stations. Fall in love again with your partner as you proclaim your love for each other in the lap of nature. From past few years there are a lot of fusion weddings happening in Kerala which makes it even more special location for a destination wedding. Plus there are lot of unique ways to host your wedding there like you can have a houseboat wedding on an actual boat! Or the traditional temple wedding or you can say I do in the hills while witnessing the nature the possibilities is endless.

Nainital

If you wish to opt for a destination wedding in an exotic location but still loves to stay touched to the Indian heritage, its culture and roots then Nainital is a great option for you. Nainital is adored by millions and is often referred as the ‘Lake District’ of India. It is a destination for all, whether you are looking for a big or a small wedding. It is well connected by the major cities and offers a lot to rejuvenate your body and soul. You can go for chilly mountain top venue or even lush green forest resorts both are equally fascinating!

Shimla

Hill stations are the newest buzz in destination wedding circuit and when it comes to hills the first location comes in our mind is definitely Shimla, the Queen of hills is a beautiful location that is known for its magnificent view, pleasant weather & dense pine forests. It’s one of the best destinations in India and perfect for a mountain wedding. Image The Himalaya’s as a backdrop for all your pre wedding rituals, Woah! sounds magical to us.
