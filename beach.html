Goa

For couples who loves beach wedding, Goa plays an amiable host with beaches spread all over the state. Good vibes of the ocean and charming tropical forests are just some of the many reasons why Goa is the ideal spot to have your destination wedding. A quick flight away from the city chaos, this place is full of beautiful wedding venues and relaxing resorts for you to have a magical fairy tale wedding celebration. Getting hitched in Goa is such an exquisite experience, and we would cater to your every wedding related dream, so that you can enjoy your special day to the fullest.

Andaman & Nicobar

Andaman & Nicobar already give us Bollywood vibes like a couple lost in a deserted island! Moreover beaches there are considered the cleanest. The whole vibe of that place is something beyond perfect. Planning a destination wedding there would be a one of kind experience for sure you can plan your wedding on several beautiful island like Havelock and port Blair are one of their most beautiful spots!

Lakshadweep

We know by now that you already love beaches and all things fancy with water. So how can you miss the best of them all Lakshadweep! The heaven on earth with a collection of total 36 islands this place is indeed a paradise where you can make all your beach wedding dreams come true! There are many hotels and resorts built very close to the azure waters and golden shining sands so that you and your guests can stay close to the beach.
